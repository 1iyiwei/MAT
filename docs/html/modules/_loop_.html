normalizeLoop

  • normalizeLoop(loop: Loop, max: number): Loop
    • Defined in loop.ts:217

    Change the loop such that:

    • No bezier is of zero length
    • All points are coerced onto a grid such that the significand of all points are reduced to 13 bits and the significant bits of all points overlap
    • All bezier points of a single curve are seperate - this prevents infinite curvature at the endpoints, etc.
    • TODO - no cusps This gives us some good guarantees for the rest of the algorithm.

    • max: number
